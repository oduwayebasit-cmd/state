GOVERNOR
DAPO ABIODUN

Prince Adedapo Oluseun Abiodun, MFR, Governor of Ogun State, Nigeria, wa
born on Sunday, 29th May, 1960 into the family of Dr Emmanuel and Mrs.
Vitoria Abiodun of Iperu-Remo, in Ikenne Local Government of Ogun State. Both
parents were teachers

He had his primary and secondary education in Nigeria and a teritary education
that started at the pretigious University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo
University), Ile-Ife, Osun State, and later continued at the State
University in Atlanta, Georgia, United States, where he graduated with a BBA in
Accounting in 1986.

Dapo began his carrer with Glock Inc. USA, where he worked as Cost
Accountant between 1989-1991. Thereafter, he returned to Nigeria and
immediately embarked on entrepreneurship voyage. He was Chairman of a
number of successful businesses, which include Crestar Hydrocarbons Limited,
OMS-Heyden Exploration and Production Limited, Limited, Heyden Petroleum Limited,
Alarmnet and Innovative Ventures Limited, and First Power Limited.
